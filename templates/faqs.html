Most frequently asked questions

Here are the most frequently asked questions you may check before getting started

I offer the identical service that Savile Row tailors do, at a fraction of the price. You deal directly with me 100% of the way through your order. My expertise took me to the highest level of Savile Row Tailoring.

Bespoke needs a minimum of 8 weeks. On average it takes 8-12 weeks. Semi Bespoke needs a 4-week minimum. On average it takes 4-5 weeks. An express service is possible for both processes. Get in touch for details.

Bespoke needs minimum of 3 fittings. Semi bespoke needs 2 fittings (initial consultation and sample try on counts as first fitting).

Each garment is priced per order. Due to the many selections the customer needs to make, this can affect the price of the suit. Price factors include the cloth for the suit, the lining, the button choice, the number of pockets internally and externally, the number of buttons, embroidery, timescale. If you have a precise idea of your order, let me know and I will price the order up as best we can. Bespoke begins at £3500 (two-piece suit) Semi Bespoke begins at £1200 (two-piece suit) How do I pay? A 50% deposit is required to begin the process. The final 50% is paid upon collection/delivery of the finished garments. BACS/online bank transfer required for payment. What is Bespoke and Semi Bespoke? Bespoke is a unique 2D pattern created by hand by Nicholas for you, using his 10 years of experience in cutting. The garments are hand sewn by expert tailors on Savile Row. There is no limit to customisation and detail. Semi Bespoke is made in a factory in Europe and mainly uses machine stitching. The measurements are taken by trying on a sample garment (a block) and details are discussed about how to improve the fit of that sample. What is your "House Style"? My house style is to flatter each persons unique body shape whilst making the most comfortable garment possible. It is not possible to fit one style to all body shapes. The process is a collaboration between tailor and customer.

The workshop at The Depot is open Wednesday - Sunday 9am-6pm by appointment only. FREE PARKING
